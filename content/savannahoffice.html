Our Savannah Office

Andrew Wilford, Senior Partner

Mr. Wilford has an AB in languages from Rutgers University (1967) and studied for three years at The Newark College of Engineering (now the NJ Institute of Technology).

He received a JD in law from Seton Hall Law School (1975).

He has been associated with KF ROSS PC since 1968 and specializes in electrical matters and mechanics as well as operating the firm.

Mr. Wilford speaks fluent French and German and has a reading knowledge of Italian and Spanish.
